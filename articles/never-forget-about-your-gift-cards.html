Never Forget About Your Gift Cards Again

Seriously, They're Hiding in Your Drawer

Let’s face it — gift cards are the boomerangs of the modern world. You get one, put it “somewhere safe,” and months later, when you’re looking for a paperclip or that one battery that still works, there it is. £25 to Amazon. Expired.

We've all been there. The holidays hit, your birthday rolls around, or your coworker — the one who still calls you "champ" — decides a generic Visa card says “I appreciate your synergy.” And you’re thrilled! Free money! …Until you lose track of it faster than your new year’s resolution to stop hoarding condiment packets.

And what’s worse? That $100 card to "Greg's Tech Emporium" now buys exactly one USB cable, because you forgot about it for 14 months. Poof — gone with the wind like your motivation after lunch.
